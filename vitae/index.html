Book Shelf

  • How an Economy Grows and Why It Crashes by Peter Schiff
    Forget about financial media, this is one of the fundamental books to read for anyone who wants to understand economic.
  • How Google Works by Eric Schmidt
  • Outliers: The Story of Success by Malcolm Gladwell
  • Principles: Life and Work by Ray Dalio
    The principles that everyone should live by, not only for people that are interested in investing. Sadly it's not widely adopted.
  • How to Be Rich by J. Paul Getty
    From the legend himself.
  • Zero to One: Notes on Startups, or How to Build the Future by Peter Thiel
  • The War of Art: Break Through the Blocks & Win Your Inner Creative Battles by Steven Pressfield
    If you create, or want to create, anything, read it daily until the words are carved in your soul.
  • Deep Work: Rules for Focused Success in a Distracted World by Cal Newport
    The best type of work explained.
  • Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind by Yuval Noah Harari
    Millions of years of human history presented in front of your eyes.
  • Animal Farm by George Orwell
  • Hackers & Painters: Big Ideas from the Computer Age by Paul Graham
